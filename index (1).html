Fitness & Health Tips for Teens

🏃‍♂️ Daily Fitness Tips

  • Start with 10–15 minutes of light stretching in the morning.
  • Do bodyweight workouts like push-ups, squats, and planks.
  • Stay consistent — small steps daily make big results.
  • Take breaks from screens and walk or do jumping jacks.
